Introduction

I am a current Junior at Cornell University studying Information Science with minors in Computer Science and Health Policy. I have always had a passion for history, and I often spend much of my time thinking (and writing a bit) about influential people and the legacy they left on the world, for better or worse. Inspired by the annual TIME 100 Most Influential People in the World, this website was created and designed to explore the 10 people I believe have had the largest impact in human history, with a brief overview of each of their lives and influence. These ten individuals are chosen based on their contributions to humanity and how different the world looked after they made their mark on history. I am a firm believer that learning from the past allows us to create a better future.
